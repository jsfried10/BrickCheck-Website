Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Overview

BrickChecker is designed with your privacy in mind. The app runs entirely on your Mac and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

What Data Does BrickChecker Store?

BrickChecker stores the following data locally on your Mac only:

  • LEGO set numbers you choose to track
  • Your LEGO.com wishlist URL (if you use wishlist sync)
  • Price data retrieved from retailers (Amazon, Target)
  • Your notification preferences and app settings

This data never leaves your computer. There is no account system, no cloud sync, and no analytics within the app.

Network Requests

BrickChecker makes network requests solely to:

  • Check current prices on supported retailers (Amazon, Target)
  • Fetch set information from the Brickset API
  • Sync your LEGO.com wishlist (if enabled)
  • Check for app updates via Sparkle

No personal data is included in these requests.

This Website

This website (brickchecker.cash) uses Google Analytics to understand visitor traffic. Google Analytics may use cookies to track page visits. No personally identifiable information is collected through the website.

Third-Party Services

BrickChecker does not share any data with third parties. The app does not contain ads, tracking SDKs, or telemetry.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
